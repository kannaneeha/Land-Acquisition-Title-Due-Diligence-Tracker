Abstract

The Land Acquisition & Title Due Diligence Tracker is a web-based application developed for Crownridge LLP to streamline the management of land acquisition activities and legal due diligence processes. The system enables users to record, monitor, and update information related to land parcels, survey numbers, title searches, encumbrance verification, and legal clearance stages through a centralized platform. It is primarily used by land acquisition teams, legal professionals, and project managers involved in property acquisition projects. By replacing manual spreadsheets and fragmented communication methods, the solution improves data accuracy, workflow transparency, reporting efficiency, and overall project visibility. After implementation, Crownridge LLP will be able to track acquisition progress more effectively, reduce administrative effort, and make faster, data-driven decisions.
