Problem Statement 

Land acquisition and title due diligence activities at Crownridge LLP are currently tracked through spreadsheets, emails, and manual document reviews. This makes it difficult to maintain a centralized view of land parcels, survey numbers, title search progress, encumbrance verification, and legal clearance stages. Manual tracking often leads to data duplication, inconsistent updates, delayed decision-making, and challenges in monitoring acquisition progress. Stakeholders spend significant time searching for information and preparing status reports. A digital tracking system is required to improve visibility, accuracy, collaboration, and workflow efficiency.

Frontend Screens

1. Land Acquisition & Title Due Diligence Entry Form

Add and update land parcel details.
Capture survey numbers, owner information, title status, encumbrance status, and legal clearance details.

2. Land Acquisition & Title Due Diligence Dashboard

Centralized view of all land parcels.
Display key metrics, recent updates, and acquisition progress.

3. Status & Workflow Tracker

Monitor parcel movement through due diligence stages.
Track title verification, encumbrance checks, approvals, and legal clearances.

4. Reports & Analytics Dashboard

Generate reports and visualize project progress.
Provide charts and summaries for management decision-making.
